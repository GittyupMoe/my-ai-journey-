Our Testing Blueprint

The 4-6 week pilot is designed to validate our four core assumptions. Each test provides critical data to inform our next steps.

Pilot Timeline (4-6 Weeks)

1

Week 1: Training & Launch

Conduct hyper-focused training. Deploy devices and begin daily usage monitoring.

2

Week 2-3: Analyze Initial Data

Analyze weekly usage trends. Begin gathering qualitative feedback and anecdotal evidence.

3

Week 4-5: Deeper Dive

Conduct focus groups to test modality sufficiency and gather detailed staff insights.

4

Week 6: Final Review

Compile all quantitative and qualitative data. Present findings and make data-driven recommendations.

Core Assumptions & Metrics

We will measure our success across four key areas to validate the effectiveness of the MVP and guide future development.

Metric: Staff Utilization Rate

Our primary goal is to validate that simplified access drives consistent use. We aim for >80% utilization in identified language-barrier encounters.

Metric: Staff Feedback Themes

Qualitative feedback will show the *real-world impact* on safety and efficiency, identifying where our service provides the most value.

Conclusion & Next Steps

This MVP pilot provides the critical, validated learning needed to de-risk our full vision. By proving our core assumptions, we will build a data-driven case for a strategic, phased rollout of the complete ImmediateConnect service, ensuring we build an effective and impactful solution for safer healthcare.
